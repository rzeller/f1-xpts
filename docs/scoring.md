# JHB F1 Pool 2026 — Scoring Rules

## Overview

Each player picks **5 drivers in ranked order** before qualifying for each race weekend. Points are earned based on how those drivers actually finish.

---

## Points per Race

### Grand Prix (25-point scale)

Points mirror the official F1 Drivers' Championship scoring for the top 10 finishers:

| Position | Points |
|----------|--------|
| 1st      | 25     |
| 2nd      | 18     |
| 3rd      | 15     |
| 4th      | 12     |
| 5th      | 10     |
| 6th      | 8      |
| 7th      | 6      |
| 8th      | 4      |
| 9th      | 2      |
| 10th     | 1      |
| 11th+    | 0      |

### Sprint Race (8-point scale)

Sprint races count in 2026 (they did **not** count in 2025). Points follow the official F1 Sprint scoring for the top 8:

| Position | Points |
|----------|--------|
| 1st      | 8      |
| 2nd      | 7      |
| 3rd      | 6      |
| 4th      | 5      |
| 5th      | 4      |
| 6th      | 3      |
| 7th      | 2      |
| 8th      | 1      |
| 9th+     | 0      |

---

## DNF / DNS Penalty

If a driver you picked **Did Not Finish (DNF)** or **Did Not Start (DNS)**, you receive **−10 points** for that pick.

This applies regardless of the reason — mechanical failure, crash, or withdrawal.

---

## Exact Position Bonus

If a driver finishes in the **exact same slot as their pick position**, you earn a **+10 point bonus** on top of their base points.

**Examples:**

- Your **Pick 1** (top driver) finishes **P1** → +10 bonus
- Your **Pick 3** finishes **P3** → +10 bonus
- Your **Pick 2** finishes **P1** → no bonus (wrong slot)

---

## Pick Deadline

Picks must be submitted before **qualifying begins** for each race. On sprint weekends, the same deadline applies to both the sprint and the grand prix (players can update picks between the sprint and the main race only if connectivity allows — e.g., during the Shanghai weekend Bill enforced an earlier deadline due to traveling in China).

---

## Standing Picks Rule

If you do not submit new picks before the deadline, your **previous race's picks carry over**. You can update at any time before the deadline.

---

## Qualifying Points

**No points are awarded for qualifying** in 2026.
